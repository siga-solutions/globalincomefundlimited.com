Our Philophoshy

The Global Income Fund philosophy is driven by innovation, prudence and a constant eye on the future, backed up by an an in-depth, bottom-up research, analysis, returns, not earth invasive and good community relationships.

The fund has a regional focus which provides valuable insight into regional trends and opportunities.

The approach of the Fund investment is a multiple proposal that follows the follwoing criterias :

  • We define a good investment as building better businesses, with important returns, a sustainable growth, with climate resiliance and strong community ties.
  • A good investment must have a value proposition that fully understands the investment and how to innovate.
  • For our investments the local presence of our group is our key to succesful investments, as well as, our diverse and inclusive team.

Our investment philosophy dictates how the team operates and what we stand for, which we believe is our key to successful investments.
